About St. Thomas Institute for Science & Technology Established in 2010 by the Mar Thoma Church Educational Society, St. Thomas Institute for Science & Technology (STIST) is a private engineering college nestled in Kattaikonam (Mar Chrysostom Nagar), Kazhakkoottam, Trivandrum . Spread across 20 acres, the campus is equipped with state-of-the-art infrastructure and is approved by AICTE and affiliated with APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University . STIST offers a wide range of B.Tech programs across core and emerging disciplines—including Civil Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Electrical & Electronics Engineering, Electronics & Communication Engineering, and Computer Science (with specializations in Data Science and AI/ML) . The institution also provides M.Tech programs, especially in Geotechnical Engineering, for advanced learning opportunities . Driven by a vision to emerge as a premier regional institution delivering quality technical education along with strong human and social values, STIST embodies its mission by creating a learner-centric environment, nurturing holistic development, and preparing future leaders in engineering . The institute benefits from the extensive educational legacy of its promotors who run multiple reputed institutions in Trivandrum, including residential schools, public schools, and a teacher’s training college .
